for our national existence and statehood Mindful of the spiritual bequest of Cyril and Methodius and the historical legacy of Great Moravia Recognizing the natural right of nations to selfdetermination Together with members of national minorities and ethnic groups living in the Slovak Republic In the interest of continuous peaceful cooperation with other democratic countries
